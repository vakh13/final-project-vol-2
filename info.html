About this space

Apartment Bakuriani is located in Villa Monte complex which is partially encroached on the forest land. Besides being the source of fresh air, the forest adds to the feeling of comfort and tranquillity. Our balcony provides the most beautiful view of the whole area of Bakuriani. The noise of Bakuriani cannot reach the property, though it is not far from the centre, located only 2km away from Kokhta Station ; 2.2 km away from “25-ianebi” and 1.7 km away from Bakuriani Park.

The space

When you hire 3-bedroom apartment, you have exclusive access to the 90 m2 apartment. Maximum occupancy is 7 guests - with 2 beds and one bunk bed in total. One bedroom downstairs - and two others upstairs. Kitchen, living room and half-bathroom are located on the first floor.
